FAQ's

1. Do reusable products really work?

Yes! Assuming you use quality products, users typically find them to be more comfortable and effective.

2. Are reusable products safe?

Quality reusable products are tested for safety and reviewed by federal health agencies, making good quality products safe to use.

3. How do I clean reusable products?

Most reusables can be cleaned using water and soap or detergent. Rinse reusable pads or period underwear in cold water and either hand or machine-wash with soap or detergent after each use. Menstrual cups can be cleaned with soap and water before use, or can be immersed for five minutes in boiling water before every cycle?
